### Autograph letter by Msgr. Angelo Livi — Prior of the Basilica of S. Lorenzo
_25 July 1984 · Florence_

![Autograph letter by the Prior of the Basilica of S. Lorenzo, Florence, 1984](https://res.cloudinary.com/ds8hmzyom/image/upload/v1777651226/pyartist/web/varie/lettera-basilica-slorenzo-firenze-1994-26.jpg)

```
INSIGNE BASILICA DI S. LORENZO
FIRENZE
```

> Dear Sir,
>
> This morning, Signora Martinelli brought me one of your works, a watercolour painted by you. I bow before the skill and technique, as our great Annigoni also praises.
>
> Signora Martinelli tells me it is a tribute, a gift from you.
>
> I hasten to thank you from the bottom of my heart. Here in S. Lorenzo, priceless artistic treasures of past centuries are kept; this painting cannot hang in the church itself, but even in my rectory it will be admired by many: there is a constant flow of people through my home.
>
> I thank you very much. I regret that during the exhibition I was away from Florence. But there will be other occasions. I wish you great fortune in the field of painting and… in life.
>
> With deep respect and a grateful heart,

_The Prior **Msgr. Angelo Livi**_
