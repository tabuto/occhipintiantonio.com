# Antonio Occhipinti

**Painter · Watercolourist · Sculptor · Portrait Artist**

> "Art makes tangible the stuff of which dreams are made." — Anonymous

---

A watercolour painter among the most refined in Italy. Born in Gela (Sicily), where he lives and works.

He has participated in numerous national and international exhibitions. His works are held in public and private collections. He has received many awards and distinctions; critics and the press have consistently shown interest in his work.

Together with the Municipality of Gela and other institutions, he has organised public works, group exhibitions and open-air painting events, hosting Italy's and the world's most celebrated artists in Gela.

Over his long artistic career he has mastered the demanding technique of watercolour. Refined and elegant, Occhipinti's watercolour captures the chromatic sensibility of the South, tempered by a careful modulation of glazes and transparencies. His aesthetic approach draws on a new expressive energy: gathering the uncompromising brilliance of Mediterranean light and softening it into tonal nuances of a highly suggestive and pleasing expressive accent.
