## «One of the finest watercolourists in Italy»

_1982 · Autograph letter · Pietro Annigoni_

![Autograph letter by Pietro Annigoni to Antonio Occhipinti, 1982](https://res.cloudinary.com/ds8hmzyom/image/upload/v1776512977/pyartist/web/personalit/lettera-autografa-p-annigoni-1982-20.jpg)

```
XVI · VII · LXXXII
PIETRO ANNIGONI
BORGO ALBIZI, 8 — TELEF. 212.438
50122 FIRENZE
```

> Dear Occhipinti,
>
> I cherish a fond memory of our meeting — a memory that is, moreover, made tangible by the fine portrait you made of me and so generously wished to give me as a gift, together with the beautiful watercolour of Gela.
>
> Looking at these two works of yours at my leisure, I notice that you are able to combine atmospherically vibrant, light-filled transparencies with a rare solidity, and — especially in the portrait — a corporeality that is so difficult to achieve in this technique. I feel I can say with complete confidence that you are one of the finest watercolourists in Italy.
>
> I thank you again and wish you much good work!

_Yours sincerely, **Pietro Annigoni**_
